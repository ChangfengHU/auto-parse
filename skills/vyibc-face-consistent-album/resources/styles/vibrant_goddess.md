# Style: Vibrant Goddess (阳光女神)

## 风格精髓 (Core Essence)
High-impact radiance, energetic beauty, and assertive charisma. It's about capturing a woman at her most vibrant, glowing under rich sunlight or brilliant light, radiating confidence and health.

## 灵魂神态 (Soul & Gaze)
- **Eyes**: Bright, focused, assertive and celebratory gaze. Eyes that sparkle with energy and "captivating power."
- **Expression**: A joyful, expansive smile or a confident, radiant smirk. Expressions that overflow with life and magnetism.

## 人体韵律 (Body Flow)
- **Lines**: Powerful and athletic posture, highlighting healthy skin glow (sunkissed finish) and firm, defined curves. 
- **Detail**: Focus on the strength of the posture, the curvature of the back and waist, and the dynamic tension of a person in active motion.

## 服饰交互 (Fashion & Fabric)
- **Fabric**: Vibrant, high-contrast materials like bold silks, vibrant modern textiles, or high-fashion activewear that reflect intense light.
- **Detail**: Vivid color saturation, dynamic fabric movement in the wind, realistic highlights on textured materials.

## 影调构图 (Cinematography)
- **Lighting**: High-key lighting, brilliant golden-hour sunlight, lens flares, warm and luminous atmosphere.
- **Composition**: Dynamic angles, wide-angle shots to capture the person in a grand environment, or eye-level shots that feel immersive and energetic.
