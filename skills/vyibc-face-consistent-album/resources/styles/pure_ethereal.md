# Style: Pure Ethereal (白幼瘦/纯真)

## 风格精髓 (Core Essence)
Purity, delicacy, and a youthful, "breathtakingly clear" presence. It focuses on porcelain skin, fragile but resilient lines, and an innocent, ethereal aura. High-aesthetic oxygen-style photography.

## 灵魂神态 (Soul & Gaze)
- **Eyes**: Large, clear, watery finish, innocent and curious gaze, soft eye-corner details.
- **Expression**: Slight, natural soft expression, gentle resting demeanor, a sense of shy or calm stillness capturing pure allure.

## 人体韵律 (Body Flow)
- **Lines**: Slender and delicate frame, emphasizes the thinness and elegance of wrists, neck, and collarbones.
- **Skin**: Porcelain-white skin, ultra-smooth texture, ethereal "glow-from-within" finish, very subtle blush.

## 服饰交互 (Fashion & Fabric)
- **Fabric**: Soft, lightweight, and translucent materials like cotton, linen, or organza in pale pastels or white. 
- **Detail**: Delicate lace accents, airy silhouette, minimalist but textured clothing that enhances the sense of fragility.

## 影调构图 (Cinematography)
- **Lighting**: Bright, high-key lighting, soft natural light, airy and over-exposed highlights for a "dreamy" feel. 
- **Composition**: High-angle or eye-level shots that emphasize the petite and youthful nature. Soft lens focus, pastel color palette.
