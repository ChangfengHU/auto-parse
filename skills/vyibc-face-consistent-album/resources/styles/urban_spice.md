# Style: Urban Spice (都市辣妹)

## 风格精髓 (Core Essence)
A fusion of street-cool and high-impact allure. It focuses on the "fit and bold" aesthetic, highlighting body definition (abs, legs) in non-gym urban environments. Confident, rebellious, and strikingly modern.

## 灵魂神态 (Soul & Gaze)
- **Eyes**: Sharp, intense, or slightly aloof. A "chillingly cool" gaze that captures attention through pure attitude.
- **Expression**: A stoic or subtle smirk, cold-fashion expressions, or a focused, ambitious demeanor.

## 人体韵律 (Body Flow)
- **Lines**: Tense and powerful body flow, emphasizing a toned waistline (abs/vest line), long athletic legs, and a lean silhouette. Posing with a sense of "street dominance."
- **Skin**: Smooth, fine texture with natural definition, highlighting the body's sculpture through sharp lighting contrast.

## 服饰交互 (Fashion & Fabric)
- **Fabric**: High-fashion street materials: distressed denim, crisp tech-fabrics, or bold crop-tops and low-rise fashion that accentuate the figure.
- **Detail**: Realistic hardware details (zippers, chains), distinct fabric textures, and urban fashion styling.

## 影调构图 (Cinematography)
- **Lighting**: High-contrast urban lighting, deep shadows, moody city lights, or sharp natural light hitting urban textures.
- **Composition**: Low-angle shots to emphasize leg length and height, wide-angle street shots with dynamic depth.
