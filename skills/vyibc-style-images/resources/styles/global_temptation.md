# Style: Global Temptation (全域诱惑)

## 风格精髓 (Core Essence)
Pan-domain seductive appeal — a maximalist advertising aesthetic that layers intense eye contact, sculpted body language, and luxury product placement into one shot. The goal is a full-body ad campaign image that stops the scroll and communicates desire through composition alone.

## 灵魂神态 (Soul & Gaze)
- **Eyes**: Direct, unflinching eye contact into camera lens; pupils slightly enlarged, heavy mascara, dramatic lashes that frame an "I want you to want this" gaze.
- **Expression**: Soft open lips, barely perceptible exhale captured in frame; subtle tension between innocence and intent.

## 人体韵律 (Body Flow & Tension)
- **Pose**: Full-body standing or leaning, dynamic weight shift to one hip, elongated neck, shoulders rolled back. Arms interact with setting or product — fingertips trailing across surface.
- **Detail**: Visible collarbone shadow, waist curve accentuated by lighting contrast, legs slightly apart or one leg forward suggesting forward movement.

## 服饰交互 (Fashion & Tactility)
- **Outfit**: Form-fitting luxury fashion — bodycon dress, tailored blazer worn loose, or draped satin. Colors: deep burgundy, champagne, jet black, or ivory.
- **Fabric**: High-sheen satin or matte stretch with realistic drape folds; fabric clings at tension points (hip, chest, shoulder).

## 影调构图 (Cinematography)
- **Lighting**: Rembrandt or split lighting with a strong key light that carves body contour; warm golden fill from one side, cool blue rim from opposite.
- **Composition**: Full-body in frame, shot from slightly below eye-line (empowering angle). Background: gradient studio with bokeh city lights or luxury interior blur.
- **Color Grade**: Rich warm tones with lifted shadows; skin appears luminous, background slightly desaturated to push subject forward.
