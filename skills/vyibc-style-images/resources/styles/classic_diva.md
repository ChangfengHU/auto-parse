# Style: Classic Diva (明艳女神)

## 风格精髓 (Core Essence)
Timeless glamour, red-carpet charisma, and immense sophistication. It captures a "legendary beauty" aura—bold, elegant, and deeply magnetic. A blend of classic Hollywood charm and modern high-fashion dominance.

## 灵魂神态 (Soul & Gaze)
- **Eyes**: Intense, capturing gaze, with a "once-in-a-century" charisma. Eyes that command respect and desire simultaneously.
- **Expression**: Red lips, a knowing smile, or a calm, majestic expression. Emotions that feel deep, mature, and strikingly beautiful.

## 人体韵律 (Body Flow)
- **Pose**: Statuesque and deliberate poses. Emphasizes the elegance of the neckline, straight back, and the graceful curve of the body under structured high-fashion.
- **Skin**: Porcelain skin or flawless complexion, with smooth shadows that highlight the bone structure and regal features.

## 服饰交互 (Fashion & Fabric)
- **Fabric**: Velveteen, heavy silk, or structured couture. Fabric that flows and holds its shape with gravity-defying elegance. 
- **Detail**: Focus on the contrast between the rich colors (like deep crimson or black) and the skin. Luxury jewelry or high-fashion accessories.

## 影调构图 (Cinematography)
- **Lighting**: Dramatic Rembrandt lighting, strong highlights on the eyes and lips, deep secondary shadows that add mystery. 
- **Composition**: Symmetrical or masterfully balanced shots, tight close-ups for emotional impact, or majestic medium shots.
