# Style: Yandere Style (病娇风格)

## 风格精髓 (Core Essence)
A paradoxical blend of fragile purity and intense, obsessive emotion. It captures a "dangerous beauty"—someone who appears delicate or broken but possesses an overwhelming, focused intensity in their gaze. Moody, mysterious, and captivatingly unsettling.

## 灵魂神态 (Soul & Gaze)
- **Eyes**: "Obsessive gaze" with a hint of madness or deep focus on the viewer. Eyes that appear "empty" yet intensely alive with emotion. Subtle shadow around the eyes.
- **Expression**: A bittersweet half-smile, a hollow blank stare, or a slight, unsettling tilt of the head. Expressions of vulnerability masked by possession.

## 人体韵律 (Body Flow)
- **Pose**: Fragile or protective poses—hugging oneself, fingers touching lips, or a slight, hunched-over stance. 
- **Detail**: Emphasizes the thinness of the frame (collarbones, wrists) to contrast with the intensity of the face. Subtle muscle tension that hints at suppressed emotion.

## 服饰交互 (Fashion & Fabric)
- **Fabric**: Dark lace, worn fabrics, or minimalist, monochrome clothing that complements a heavy atmosphere. 
- **Detail**: Messy or slightly disheveled hair, fabric that drapes in a way that suggests a lack of care for the outside world, focusing on the inner emotion.

## 影调构图 (Cinematography)
- **Lighting**: Low-key lighting, heavy shadows, cool tones (blues/desaturated), or a single harsh light source that creates dramatic contrast.
- **Composition**: Tight frame, tilted "Dutch" angles to create a sense of unease, or silhouettes that isolate the person in a shadowy environment.
