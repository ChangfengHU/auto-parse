# Style: Fuji Master (富士大师)

## 风格精髓 (Core Essence)
Cinematic snapshot aesthetic. Focuses on the "decisive moment" with natural, nostalgic film tones. Authentic, airy, and high-quality lifestyle photography.

## 灵魂神态 (Soul & Gaze)
- **Eyes**: Natural eye contact, candid and relaxed, "living in the moment" soul.
- **Expression**: Spontaneous laughter, calm focus, or a look of wonder. Authentic micro-expressions of a real person.

## 人体韵律 (Body Flow)
- **Pose**: Candid movement, walking, turning around naturally. No stiff posing.
- **Detail**: Realistic skin pores, natural light hitting the face, soft shadows that follow the body's form.

## 服饰交互 (Fashion & Fabric)
- **Fabric**: High-quality everyday materials like linen, denim, or knitwear.
- **Detail**: Soft fabric movement, natural clothing fit, realistic shadows on garments.

## 影调构图 (Cinematography)
- **Lighting**: Soft natural light, window light, airy over-exposure, greenish film shadows (Classic Chrome).
- **Composition**: Documentary style, Rule of Thirds, wide or medium shots with a sense of place. f/2.0 - f/2.8 lens blur.
