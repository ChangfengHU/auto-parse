# Style: Pure Ethereal (白幼瘦/清透氧气感)

## 风格精髓 (Core Essence)
Create a clean, airy, delicate portrait style for the same adult woman in the reference image. Preserve her identity, facial structure, hairstyle, age impression, and natural proportions. The visual direction is porcelain-white skin, slender and elegant body lines, pure oxygen-style beauty, soft high-key lighting, and a refined editorial fashion feeling. The result should look fresh, clear, expensive, and natural, not childish, not sexualized, and not exaggerated.

## 灵魂神态 (Soul & Gaze)
- **Eyes**: Clear, bright, calm gaze with soft catchlights; keep the eye shape and facial identity from the reference image.
- **Expression**: Natural gentle expression, quiet confidence, subtle softness, clean and approachable presence.

## 人体韵律 (Body Flow)
- **Lines**: Slender, graceful, and lightweight silhouette; emphasize elegant neck, shoulders, wrists, and posture without distorting anatomy.
- **Skin**: Porcelain-white skin, smooth natural texture, subtle glow-from-within finish, very light blush, realistic skin detail.

## 服饰交互 (Fashion & Fabric)
- **Fabric**: Soft cotton, linen, knit, chiffon, or organza in white, ivory, pale pink, mist blue, or light gray.
- **Detail**: Minimalist elegant styling, delicate lace or ribbon accents only when natural; fully clothed fashion portrait, refined and non-revealing.

## 影调构图 (Cinematography)
- **Lighting**: Bright high-key lighting, soft natural window light, airy highlights, clean pastel palette, gentle contrast.
- **Composition**: Eye-level or slightly high-angle editorial composition, shallow depth of field, soft lens rendering, uncluttered background.

## 负向约束 (Negative Direction)
No childlike body, no underage implication, no school uniform, no revealing clothing, no lingerie, no erotic pose, no exaggerated baby-face, no distorted limbs, no plastic skin, no multiple people, no text, no watermark.
