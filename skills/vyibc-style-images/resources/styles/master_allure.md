# Style: Master Allure (全域魅惑)

## 风格精髓 (Core Essence)
Intense emotional pull, sophisticated charisma, and dynamic physical tension. It's about a "story in the eyes" and a "presence in the pose," transcending any specific scene. Focused on high-fashion aesthetic charm.

## 灵魂神态 (Soul & Gaze)
- **Eyes**: Heavy-lidded, lingering gaze, "soul-pulling" intensity, subtle emotional tension.
- **Expression**: A trace of a breathy half-smile, slightly parted lips with a natural sheen, expressions that convey confidence and capturing allure.

## 人体韵律 (Body Flow & Tension)
- **Pose**: Torsional body language, emphasizing the natural S-curve through gentle twisting or weight shifting. 
- **Detail**: Focus on the tension in the neck muscles, the curve of the waist, and the subtle shadows cast by the collarbone. Realistic skin texture with a soft sheen.

## 服饰交互 (Fashion & Tactility)
- **Fabric**: Luxurious textures like silk, satin, or fine lace that drape and reflect light realistically. 
- **Detail**: Realistic fabric folds, soft shadows where clothes meet skin, emphasizing a high-fashion aesthetic. 

## 影调构图 (Cinematography)
- **Lighting**: Low-key lighting, moody shadows, rim lights that trace the body's silhouette, volumetric atmosphere.
- **Composition**: Masterful use of close-ups and low-angle shots for visual impact. Shallow depth of field (f/1.4 - f/2.0).
